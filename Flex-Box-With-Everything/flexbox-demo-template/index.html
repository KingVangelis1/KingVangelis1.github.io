Who is Skelagon?

Here is my finished version of Skelagon that I created with Zbrush. The version that I am happy with. As you can see it is very different from the concept. I also have expereince with unity, 3D max, Maya and Photoshop.

Now there was quite a lot of skills needed to make this. The first was I needed was how to paint color onto the model. The second was most of this is human skeleton work so I had to do a lot of referencing to make sure it matched up with how that normally works. The skull and the wings I had no reference for apart from what I think it should look like.

Now I individually make the pieces themselves so they had a huge poly count, so I had to majorly reduce the ploys without losing detail so it didn't need too much power. The last thing was I needed to create a turntable for this. Now the only criticism I had when I finished this was he wanted a turntable of just the skull because he found that to be the most impressive.
