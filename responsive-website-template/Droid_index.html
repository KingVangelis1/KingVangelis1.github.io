The best non verbal droid

For all his faithfulness and heroics over the years, R2-D2 deserves the top spot, from being Anakin Skywalker's right-hand to aiding the Resistance when the time is right. He has the best elements of every droid mixed into one, from his strong companionship to his constant sassiness that would surely need censorship if Binary was able to be more easily translated. R2 is a key agent in the Rebellion's first major win against the Empire in the Galactic Civil War, recruiting both Luke and Obi-Wan to the cause. The Star Wars galaxy simply wouldn't be the same without R2, and thankfully, there's still plenty of time left for R2 to continue making his galactic impact as new stories are told.
