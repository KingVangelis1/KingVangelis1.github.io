Why we think this darker version exists.

By Lucas Wold

So there are a lot of hints in this game this Darker Version exists.

We will go over the biggest things we found to prove this point.

There of course many more examples but these are just the ones we found.

This Character is called Skelagon. He is the biggest example of a draker version.

Because he isn't in the gameplay. Just cut-scenes.

Sounds like they cut him out of most of it because he was too intense of a character.

I mean look at him. He looks too intense for a family game.

This next part is an important plot point that doesn't get expalined.

Skelagon infects Hugo with a spirit of some kind.

This makes Hugo do evil stuff sometimes. Which is something he normally won't do.

A good example of that spirit taking over is this scene.

This scene he grabs the other villain he works with by the neck.

Hugo then flies upwards with him to a height where it would kill him if dropped

He then gets out his sword then drops him.

If there was a darker version, it could be Hugo uses his sword then drops him.

For this scene Hugo is angry with the same villain he works with for failing him.

So Hugo gets out his sword. He then says "You need to be punished for failing."

This part Hugo hurts the other villain with his sword.

This scene goes black and white because the screen becomes darker.

Then right before Hugo stabs the guy the screen goes fully black and we hear a scream.

If this wasn't censored it would most likely show him stabing him.

The other villain has an armcast the rest of the game though so this scene isn't pointless.

These 3 are the biggest examples of this game being censored that we found.

There of course are more examples of this. There is way more than this.

These 3 are just the biggest ones to us.

You can search the game itself to see way more of this. There is a lot if you look for it.
