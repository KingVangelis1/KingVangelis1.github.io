What do we do at Twilight Insights?

By Lucas Wold

What do we do here? We try to find a deeper side of media to show you the stuff they try to hide.

So we try to take typically family friendly stuff and show you it isn't that simple.

Because of course things are never as it seems.

Everything has more grey parts then black and white parts.

Now since we show the darker side of things so much we also do the reverse.

That means we take stuff that is very dark and try to lighten it up.

So we try to highlight the postive elements of really dark things. Like Game Of Thrones.

Where there is light there must be shadow. So we don't upset the balance we give both.

So here we show you a balance of showing you the dark and the light.

Because things are never as balck and white as they seem.

There is always more shades of grey in there.

No matter how colorful something is, almost always it is not that simple.
